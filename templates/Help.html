1.Data source and used websites/softwares

We defined the regulatory roles of PTM on Protein-Protein Interactions1,2,3,4,5,6: 1)Enabling: Increase affinity and 2)Disabling: Decrease affinity. We extracted the functional PTM sites and associated literature from Uniprot①, PTMD②, PTMfunc③ and PhosphoSitePlus④ databases. We also downloaded the relevant literature using PubMed⑤ database by searching the following keywords and the combinations: human, protein, bind, associate, enable, interact, interaction, inhibit, disable, prevent, dissociate, site, PTM, phosphorylation, acetylation, methylation, sumoylation, ubiquitylation and glycosylation. Then, we checked the full-text of above nearly 3,600 papers carefully to obtain complete experimental evidence, which included regulatory PTM sites and types, interacting proteins, detection methods, associated diseases and co-localization.

2.Browse

Users can easily specify PTM and gene to retrieve the results.


3.Search

PTMint database provides two search ways for users: 1)Quick search; 2)Advanced search.
The hit results will be shown in the tabular format. And detailed results can be obtained by clicking "Show" buttons.


4.Results

Click the "Show" button, the detailed results will be shown in a new page, which includes six sections:

(1)Overview, which provides protein basic information, full-length protein structure (Predicted by AlphaFold) and external links.


(2)Protein features. Disorder analysis: Exceed 0.5 means disorder.


(3)PTM on Protein-Protein Interactions, which be composed of two parts: complete experimental evidence, structure and site annotation.


(4)Interaction network. Blue square: Gene; Green circle: Int_gene.
(5)Importance score, which be sorted by descending order. Higher score means higher important role in disease process and potential drug targets.


(6)Complex analysis. Users can easily manipulate and switch structures. Interaction analysis are also provided.
HB: Hydrogen bond; HP: Hydrophobic interaction; ELE: Electrostatic interaction.
All complex structures, interfaceResidues and interaction analysis can be downloaded by clicking the 'Download' button.
