※ Introduction

Here we constructed a database of PTMint (Post Translational Modifications that are associated with Protein-Protein Interactions) that contains manually curated complete experimental evidence of the PTM effecting on protein-protein interactions (Enabling and Disabling). Currently, the first version of PTMint encompasses 2,439 non-redundant PTM sites in 1,150 proteins affecting 2,320 protein-protein pairs involving 357 diseases. Various annotations were systematically integrated, such as protein sequence, structure properties and protein complex analysis. We believed this database can help both biologists and bioinformaticians to gain a better understanding of disease development, diagnosis and drug discovery for PTM affection.
